Vision and Mission:

At Sand River Community Farm, we long to recover our deeply human capacity for mutually sustaining relationships, both human to human, and human to all that is not human. We grow and glean food which is offered as a gift to anyone who is hungry for any reason. Through practices of reckless generosity and intergenerational table fellowship, the Farm sparks a neighborly conversation about how we might feed one another without buying and selling food.
